7th International IP workshop

We are happy to announce that the 7th International IP workshop will take place between 28th - 30th May 2024 in the heart of Lund, Sweden.
Please save the dates!

Lund is well connected to the European train system. The nearest well-connected international airport is Copenhagen/Denmark, and there are a limited number of international flights to Malmö Airport.

Since Lund is usually very populated with tourist by this time of the year, we strongly recommend an early booking of the hotel. A list of hotels can be found here.

More information will be sent out in autumn of this year. We are looking forward to welcoming all of you in Sweden next year

Venue

The venue is the beautiful AF Borgen, in the heart of Lund.

Abstract Submission

Please use the provided template to submit your abstract. The abstract should be maximum two pages in total. The deadline is February 28th 2024! All abstracts will be openly available and published on the website after the workshop.

Send the abstract as pdf (not password protected) InternationalIPWorkshop@gmail.com. State the title in the email subject.

Sponsorship

We have set up three main sponsorship packages, each coming with more perks and levels of exposure to promote your company/organization.

Program

There will be 4 session help during the conference and 1 general session. The discussions will be focused around the sessions.

  • Advances in mineral exploration
  • Transition lab to field and uncertainty management
  • Environmental session: Contamination/Monitoring/Transportation processes
  • Improvements in instrumentation and data processing
  • General session

A first version of the program will be available by the end of April.
